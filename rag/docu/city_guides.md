## Tokyo, Japan
- Best time to visit: March-May (cherry blossoms) or Oct-Nov (autumn colors).
- Pack: comfortable walking shoes, portable WiFi or eSIM, cash (many places are cash-only).
- Get a Suica/Pasmo card for trains immediately at the airport.
- Budget tip: convenience store (konbini) meals are cheap and high quality.

## Bangkok, Thailand
- Best time to visit: Nov-Feb (cooler, drier).
- Pack: light breathable clothing, modest clothing for temple visits (cover shoulders/knees).
- Use Grab app for taxis instead of street hailing.

## Paris, France
- Best time to visit: April-June or Sept-Oct.
- Pack: comfortable shoes for cobblestones, a light jacket even in summer evenings.
- Buy a Navigo Easy card for metro travel.

## Dubai, UAE
- Best time to visit: Nov-March (extremely hot April-Oct).
- Pack: light clothing but also modest options for malls/mosques.
- Friday is part of the weekend; some businesses operate on reduced hours.

## General Packing Checklist
- Universal travel adapter
- Photocopies of passport + visa
- Travel insurance documents
- Medication with prescription copy
- Power bank for phone
