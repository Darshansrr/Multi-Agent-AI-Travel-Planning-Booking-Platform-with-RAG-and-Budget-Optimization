## Japan
- Indian passport holders generally need a visa for tourism.
- Processing time: ~5-7 working days via VFS Global.
- Required: passport, photo, bank statement (last 6 months), itinerary, hotel bookings.

## Thailand
- Indian citizens can get a Visa on Arrival (VoA) for stays up to 15 days.
- Alternatively, e-Visa available online before travel.

## Schengen (Europe)
- Single Schengen visa covers 27 European countries.
- Apply through the embassy of the country you'll spend the most nights in.
- Travel insurance with minimum 30,000 EUR coverage is mandatory.

## UAE (Dubai)
- Indian passport holders with a valid US/UK/Schengen visa or green card may get visa on arrival.
- Otherwise, e-Visa can be applied online, processing ~3-4 days.
